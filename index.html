About

CRMA!
“Music for the hours when the world goes quiet and the mind gets loud.”

CRMA! is the sonic journal of an artist who finds beauty in the weight of the night. Rooted in atmospheric R&B and lo-fi soul, the sound is built for solitary reflection — airy vocals drifting over slow-burning beats, lyrics that sit heavy with persistence and emotional truth.

Drawing from the emotional rawness of Dijon, the genre-defying intimacy of Frank Ocean, and the textural experimentation of mk.gee, CRMA! filters these influences through a deeply personal lens. With the release of Misery Avenue, that vision crystallizes — eight tracks of late-night vulnerability distilled into raw, cinematic storytelling. Each song is a room you walk through alone — melancholic, honest, but quietly resilient.

The production is minimalist by design, giving space for the voice and the silence between words to carry the weight. Based in the space between midnight and dawn, CRMA! doesn't chase the light. The music lives in the shadows, and that's exactly where it belongs.
