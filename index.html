About

CRMA!
“Music for the hours when the world goes quiet and the mind gets loud.”

CRMA! is the sonic journal of an artist who finds beauty in the weight of the night. Rooted in atmospheric R&B and lo-fi soul, the sound is built for solitary reflection — airy vocals drifting over slow-burning beats, lyrics that sit heavy with persistence and emotional truth.

With the release of Misery Avenue, CRMA! distills late-night vulnerability into eight tracks of raw, cinematic storytelling. Each song is a room you walk through alone — melancholic, honest, but quietly resilient. The production is minimalist by design, giving space for the voice and the silence between words to carry the weight.

Based in the space between midnight and dawn, CRMA! doesn't chase the light. The music lives in the shadows, and that's exactly where it belongs.
